Route Optimization

Route optimization is the process of finding the most efficient route for a vehicle or fleet of vehicles to travel. This can be done by taking into account various factors such as distance, traffic, road conditions, and vehicle capacity.

Autocomplete

Route autocomplete is a feature that suggests possible routes based on the destination entered and starting point entered by the user. It is often used in navigation and route optimization software.
